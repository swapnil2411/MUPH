Our International Relations Department will ensure seamless exchange of information regarding treatment modality, costs involved, probable outcomes, duration of stay (both in and out of the hospital) etc.

We at Muljibhai Patel Urological Hospital understand that undergoing medical treatment in a foreign country can be quiet challenging both emotionally and financially, this is why we aim at offering services that will ease your journey and make your experience worthwhile.

Enquire Now
  • To begin your journey with us, it is very important to get your medical history and recent reports reviewed by our specialists. You can simply forward all the scanned reports to our official email.
  • Our specialised team of international patient coordinators make sure that all your queries are answered within 24 hours.
  • After receiving your approval for undergoing the treatment, you will be briefly informed about the treatment procedure and the estimated cost expenditure.
  • To make it easier for the patients, we offer different payment options.
  • Our representatives help you with your VISA formalities and we provide an official Visa invitation to the patients and their attendants.

Follow-Up Care & Services

In the case of follow-ups, you can contact our specialists via mail, phone or fax, and they will guide you accordingly. Once the treatment is over, our doctors offer brief evaluation and instructions related to post-operative care. You may be asked to come for follow-ups to make sure that there is no complication. Once the doctors are sure, you will be given a medical document or Fit Fly documentation so that you can easily fly back home.
