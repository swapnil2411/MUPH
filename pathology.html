Pathology

Pathology is a medical specialty that involves the study and diagnosis of diseases. Our pathologists use various techniques to determine the cause and progression of illnesses and work closely with the doctors for effective treatment planning.

What we do

Pathology plays a crucial role in ensuring effective disease treatment. Our pathology laboratory is well-equipped with state-of-the-art equipment capable of conducting a comprehensive range of tests at reasonable rates. The department is headed by experienced senior pathologists who work alongside skilled technicians to deliver precise and reliable diagnoses. We stand out by implementing error-free systems to guarantee accuracy in our results.
