Hospitalisation Options

We are having well ventilated rooms of all categories from General ward to VIP room. All rooms are having intercom facility, fridge, A/C and television. All In-patients receive treatment by team of Doctors and nurses, available round the clock under the supervision and guidance of senior consultant Doctor along with polite and courteous para-medical staff. All wards have medical facilities of portable x-ray, ECG services, bed site sample collection from own pathology, round the clock ambulance, 24 hours Generator backup, chairs for attendants etc.. We also provide 24X 7 Pharmacy for IPD patients. Hospital premises are protected with 24 X 7 Security Guards services along with CCTV Camera to provide safe environment in Hospital. Waste disposal is done as per the established rules (Biomedical Waste Management and Handling Rules) utmost care is taken to keep the premises neat and clean.
