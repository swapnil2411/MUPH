Our Doctors

Today, I feel proud that MPUH is ranked amongst the world’s top 10 institutions for kidney stones and our Managing Trustee, Dr Mahesh Desai, among the world’s top 10 kidney stone experts – a ranking compiled by Experts cape, an independent organisation which uses an objective algorithm to identify the world’s top experts in various medical fields.One of the most heartwarming things about MPUH is when satisfied patients become our ambassadors and are inspired to give back. They not only send more patients from their villages but also raise funds for the hospital. It is a wonderful example of how the circle of gratitude is expanded to touch more lives.

- Rohit J Patel
