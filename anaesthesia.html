Anaesthesia

Anaesthesia is a boon for surgery, allowing for complex medical procedures in the absence of pain. It involves the administration of drugs that can block pain sensations and/or cause unconsciousness, as well as the monitoring of vital signs such as blood pressure and heart rate.

What we do

We prioritise patient safety and comfort during surgical procedures. That's why we have a dedicated team of expert anaesthesiologists who are available to provide support for every surgery. They are highly trained and experienced in administering the appropriate type and dosage of anaesthesia required for each patient's unique medical history and the specific surgical procedure being performed.
