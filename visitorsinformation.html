Welcome to our Home:
A Guide for Visitors

We give equal importance to Visitor Care and convenience as well. If you are visiting our hospital then this capsule is for you to get a brief on what we offer to our guests. Equipment to delivering personalized care to patients, we leave no stone unturned to deliver you simply the best.

Timing for having a Breakfast is 8.00 am to 11.00 am. Lunch timing is 12.30 pm to 02.30 pm & the Dinner timing is 7.30 pm to 9.30 pm.

Visitors are allowed to see the patients only during the specified visiting hours. Please be considerate and keep your visit brief if the patient is in pain or in need of rest. Two visitors are allowed at a time to visit the patient between 11:00 am to 12:00 noon & 5:00 pm to 7:00 pm in Wards. Two visitors only are allowed to visit the patient between 11:00 am to 12:00 noon & 4:00 pm to 5:00 pm in ICUs.
