Meet our Doctors

At MPUH, we bring our vision of delivering world-class care to all through the dedication and expertise of our doctors. Our panel comprises some of the most distinguished urologists, nephrologists, radiologists, pathologists and anaesthesiologists in India, who possess unparalleled experience in their respective fields and are proficient in the latest medical technologies.
